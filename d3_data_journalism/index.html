Health Risks vs. Poverty, Age, & Income



Correlations Discovered Between Variables

There is a slight positive correlation between being in poverty and obesity, being in poverty and smoking, and being in poverty and lack of healthcare.

There is no correlation between age and obesity. There is a positive correlation between age and smoking, and there is a slight negative correlation between age and lack of healthcare.

There is a negative correlation between household income and obesity and household income and smoking. There is a slight negative correlation between household income and lack of healthcare.
